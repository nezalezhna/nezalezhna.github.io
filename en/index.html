Our charity fund provides prompt currently required help to the military and civilians in the combat areas in the East and South of Ukraine.

Charity Organization “Charity Fund Nezalezhna” provides all the necessary for the following:

  • 35 military units
  • 12 hospitals
  • 14 communes
  • 5 civil and military administrations

We buy automobiles for the front lines, repair cars and APCs, supply provisions, ammunition, medicines, heating devices, drones, machinery.

We personally deliver supplies to the positions and people they are requested by. All needs and the transfer of the required things are confirmed with reports. We are helping now for our INDEPENDENT future. We value lives of those people who fight for us in the hell.

We will stand up and win, because we are all together.
